Buddy History


  The Estimator's Buddy began 50 years ago, when Sherman C. "Bud" Jones, in collaboration with Elmer Gove, established the Power & Process Division at Owens-Corning Fiberglass, Kansas City, MO. In 1974, Bud launched Power Insulation of Minneapolis, MN, where he continued to refine The Estimator's Buddy for another 25 years.
  From Guam to Alaska, hospitals to pipelines and nuclear power plants, Bud insulated dozens of projects, of all sizes. He was awarded almost every job he bid. The cornerstone of our system is the most comprehensive and realistic set of labor and production rates for industrial and commercial insulation construction in existence. Bud further enhanced The Estimator's Buddy with factors to account for weather and local labor variables, which will affect production, and accompanying support. Combined with Job Control, Unit Prices, and CAD integration, no other system is so complete.
